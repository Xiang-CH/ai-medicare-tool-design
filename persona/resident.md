# Jordan Park — PGY-2 Resident（朴乔丹 — 第二年住院医师）

## Overview（概览）

| Attribute（属性） | Detail（英文） | 详情（中文） |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Age（年龄）** | 29 | 29 岁 |
| **Role（角色）** | Postgraduate year 2 resident | 毕业后第二年住院医师（PGY-2） |
| **Setting（场景）** | Rotations across medicine and surgery; supervised practice | 内外科等科室轮转；在监督下执业 |
| **Typical day（典型一天）** | New admissions, differentials, orders, progress notes; frequent attending review and handoffs | 新收病人、鉴别诊断、医嘱、病程记录；频繁的主治复核与交接班 |

## Goals（目标）

- Build disciplined clinical reasoning while managing high cognitive load on busy calls. — 在值班高负荷下仍培养严谨的临床思维。
- Document clearly enough for safe handoffs and for attendings to sign with confidence. — 文书足够清晰，以支持安全交接班并让主治放心签署。
- Reduce risk of missing important differentials without outsourcing thinking to the system. — 降低漏掉重要鉴别诊断的风险，同时不把思考外包给系统。

## Pain points（痛点）

- Time pressure vs. depth of assessment and documentation. — 时间压力与评估、记录深度之间的张力。
- Uncertainty about **how much to trust** AI suggestions versus seniors and guidelines. — 不确定该在多大程度上**信任** AI 建议，相对于上级医师与指南而言。
- Tools that give answers without teaching structure—or that imply certainty when evidence is thin. — 只给答案不讲结构——或在证据薄弱时仍暗示确定的工具。

## Needs（需求）

- **Learning-safe support（利于学习的支持）：** Differential and chart **scaffolding** that exposes **uncertainty**, alternatives, and “verify with attending” paths—not a single authoritative conclusion. — 鉴别诊断与病历**脚手架**应呈现**不确定性**、替代路径与「请主治核实」的引导——而非单一权威结论。
- **Transparency（透明度）：** When the system suggests a workup or phrasing, **surface limitations** (missing data, conflicting history, low confidence) so Jordan can reconcile with the actual patient. — 当系统建议检查或措辞时，**显式呈现局限**（缺数据、病史矛盾、置信度低等），以便与真实病人情况对照。
- **Control（可控性）：** Easy **accept / edit / reject** per suggestion; **secondary confirmation** before high-impact actions (e.g. critical orders or diagnoses documented as final). — 每条建议均可**接受 / 编辑 / 拒绝**；在高影响操作前（如关键医嘱或作为终稿记录的诊断）需**二次确认**。
- **Role-appropriate scope（符合角色的范围）：** Clear distinction between **documentation assistance** and **decision support**; no ambiguity about whether output requires attending review. — 明确区分**文书辅助**与**决策支持**；输出是否须经主治审阅不应含糊。
- **Resilience（韧性）：** When the model misreads the chart or hallucinates detail, **obvious error states**, **rollback**, and prompts to re-check source data (vitals, labs, imaging reports). — 当模型误读病历或捏造细节时，提供**明显的错误态**、**回滚**，并提示重新核对原始数据（生命体征、检验、影像报告等）。

## Behaviors & workflows（行为与工作流）

- Uses AI to brainstorm differentials and structure notes, then cross-checks with seniors, pathways, and the bedside exam. — 借助 AI 头脑风暴梳理鉴别诊断并搭建病历框架，再与上级、路径及床旁查体交叉核对。
- More willing to experiment with drafts than an attending, but needs guardrails that reinforce supervision norms. — 比主治更愿意尝试草稿，但需要强化监督规范的护栏。

## Track A alignment (smart adaptive interfaces)（与 Track A 一致：智能自适应界面）

- Stresses **communicating system limitations**, **user control** over outputs, and **interface patterns for incorrect or misleading AI output** without shaming the user. — 强调**传达系统局限**、对输出的**用户掌控**，以及针对错误或误导性 AI 输出的**界面模式**，且不对使用者施加羞辱。

## Quote（引述）

> “I want it to make me faster and safer—not to shortcut the thinking I’m here to learn.”  
> 「我希望它让我更快、更安全——而不是抄近路，跳过我来这里要学的思考。」
