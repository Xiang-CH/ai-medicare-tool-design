## Goals（目标）

- Complete timely, legally sound nursing documentation that matches what was assessed and done. — 及时完成合法合规的护理记录，并与实际评估与操作一致。
- Reduce contradictory or duplicate entries across nursing and medical notes. — 减少护理记录与医疗记录之间的矛盾或重复。
- Communicate clearly with physicians when clinical picture or orders are ambiguous. — 当病情或医嘱含糊时，能与医师清晰沟通。

## Pain points（痛点）

- Documentation load competes with direct patient care and coordination. — 文书负担与床旁照护、协调工作争抢时间。
- High sensitivity to documentation that **infers** assessments or medical decisions Riley did not perform. — 对**推断**出本人未执行的评估或医疗决策的文书高度敏感。
- Frustration when systems mis-parse timelines, medications, or vitals—creating extra rework and trust issues. — 系统误读时间线、用药或生命体征时令人沮丧——带来额外返工与信任问题。

## Needs（需求）

- **Scope by role（按角色限定范围）：** AI assistance must respect **nursing documentation boundaries**—no auto-filled medical diagnoses or physician-only conclusions in nursing notes without explicit human intent. — AI 辅助须尊重**护理文书边界**——未经明确人工意图，不得在护理记录中自动填入医疗诊断或仅应由医师书写的结论。
- **Control（可控性）：** Granular **accept / edit / reject** for each paragraph or field; **undo** after accidental acceptance; no bulk apply without review. — 按段落或字段细粒度**接受 / 编辑 / 拒绝**；误采纳后可**撤销**；未经审阅不得一键批量套用。
- **Transparency & limits（透明度与限制）：** Clear labels when text is **AI-suggested**, **unverified**, or **incomplete**; surfacing when the chart is **missing data** needed for a safe suggestion. — 对**AI 建议**、**未核实**或**不完整**的文本有清晰标注；当病历**缺数据**导致无法安全建议时予以提示。
- **Safety（安全）：** Prominent checks for **consistency** (e.g. pain score vs. narrative, fall risk vs. mobility plan) without inventing missing assessments. — 突出**一致性**检查（如疼痛评分与叙述、跌倒风险与活动计划），但不编造缺失的评估内容。
- **Failure handling（故障处理）：** When the system fails or misinterprets data, **non-destructive recovery**—preserve Riley’s draft, show what failed, and suggest manual completion paths. — 系统故障或误读数据时**非破坏性恢复**——保留草稿、说明失败原因，并提示手动补全路径。

## Behaviors & workflows（行为与工作流）

- Primarily uses AI for **structuring narrative**, **template completion**, and **gap checks**; escalates clinical ambiguity to the medical team. — 主要将 AI 用于**叙述结构**、**模板补全**与**缺项检查**；临床含糊处上报医疗团队。
- Prefers tools that reinforce accountability: Riley remains the **named author** of nursing content. — 偏好强化责任的工具：护理内容的**署名作者**仍是本人。

## Track A alignment (smart adaptive interfaces)（与 Track A 一致：智能自适应界面）

- Highlights **capability boundaries**, **error recovery** after wrong or misleading output, and **proactive communication of limitations** so staff are not misled about reliability. — 突出**能力边界**、错误/误导输出后的**错误恢复**，以及**主动说明局限**，避免员工误判系统可靠度。

> 「只要能让安全写病历更快，我都愿意用——前提是别替我写出我没亲自评估过的话。」
